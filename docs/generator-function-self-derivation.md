## Level 0 — The Three Atoms

Apply the method's own Step 2 (Dimension) to the six steps. What dimensions differentiate them?

**Direction:** Top-down (whole → parts) vs Bottom-up (parts → whole) vs Lateral (across the space)

**Mode:** Analytical (taking apart) vs Synthetic (putting together) vs Generative (producing new)

**Completeness:** Exhaustive (cover everything) vs Selective (choose what matters)

**Object:** Operating on things vs relationships vs absences

Each step tested against dimensions:

| Step | Direction | Mode | Completeness | Object |
|------|-----------|------|-------------|--------|
| Decompose | Top-down | Analytical | Exhaustive | Things |
| Dimension | Lateral | Analytical | Exhaustive | Relationships |
| Traverse | Lateral | Synthetic | Exhaustive | Relationships |
| Gap | Bottom-up | Analytical | Selective | Absences |
| Compose | Bottom-up | Synthetic | Selective | Things |
| Loop | Top-down | Generative | Selective | Things |

**Traverse** collapses — it's Dimension applied exhaustively. Kill it. It's a composition: `apply(Dimension, exhaustive)`.

**Loop** collapses — it's Gap applied to the output, feeding Decompose. Kill it. It's a composition: `Gap(output) → Decompose`.

**Four survivors: Decompose, Dimension, Gap, Compose.**

Now decompose the survivors:

**Decompose** requires: perceive that parts exist (distinguish), then separate them. Distinguish is more fundamental.

**Dimension** is Distinguish applied to properties rather than parts. "What makes A different from B?" is Distinguish applied to relationships.

**Gap** is Distinguish applied to absences. "What should be here but isn't?"

**Compose** requires: connect parts (relate), then judge which connections are meaningful (select).

**Three irreducible atoms:**

| Atom | What it does | Without it |
|------|-------------|------------|
| **Distinguish** | Perceive difference | No parts, no dimensions, no gaps — undifferentiated whole |
| **Relate** | Perceive connection | Isolated atoms, no structure, no composition |
| **Select** | Choose what matters | Infinite distinctions, no focus, no meaning |
